I am a Machine Learning Engineer at H. Lee Moffitt Cancer Center and Research Institute, working at the intersection of artificial intelligence and oncology. My research focuses on:

* **Medical Image Segmentation** - Automated CT segmentation with uncertainty quantification
* **Uncertainty Quantification** - Bayesian neural networks and ensemble methods for reliable AI
